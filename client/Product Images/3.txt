Disc Plough (Domestic)
Ploughs
 It is directly mounted to the tractors.
 It can be use easily at rocky and rooted areas.
 It is used to open the new fields and to process the stony areas.
 It is specially useful in hard and dry trashy land conditions and in soils where scouring is a major problem.
 The disc plough is designed to work in medium & light soil for basic functions such as soil breaking, soil raising, soil turning and soil mixing.
